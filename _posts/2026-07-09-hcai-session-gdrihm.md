# HCAI Session at the GDR IHM Days

We’re happy to invite you to our upcoming HCAI (Human-Centered AI) Working Group session during the [GDR IHM days](https://gdr-ihm.cnrs.fr/journees-gdr-ihm-2026/). 

General Information:
- When: 9 July 2026, 9h30 to 12h
- Where (in person): 20, avenue George Sand, 93210 La Plaine St-Denis
- Where (online): https://visio.numerique.gouv.fr/ngh-okyt-cgt
