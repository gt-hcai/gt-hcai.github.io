Welcome

The HCAI Working Group focuses on Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence within the GDR IHM. Its mission is to explore and connect research efforts across France that address the design and analysis of AI from a human-centered perspective. This includes understanding and shaping how AI systems interact with individuals and groups, as well as advancing systems that are explainable, controllable, accountable, and trustworthy.
