--- layout: home author_profile: true --- The GT HCAI is the working group on Human-centered Artificial Intelligence within the GDR IHM. The objective of this working group is to study and bring together research activities in France focused on designing and analyzing AI from a human-centered perspective. This involves not only understanding and designing how AI systems interact with individual users and groups, but also developing systems that are more explainable, controllable, accountable, and trustworthy.
